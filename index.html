Our Reserves

Complete transparency. All reserves are real-time verified on-chain.

How We Maintain Reserves

Cold Storage Security

95% of customer assets are stored in secure multi-signature cold wallets. Reserve holdings are verified through secure proof-of-reserve checks.

  • Multi-signature wallets (3-of-5)
  • Hardware wallet backups
  • Geographically distributed

Real-Time Verification

All reserve balances are verified directly from the blockchain. No manual claims - just cryptographic proof.

  • On-chain verification 24/7
  • Proof-of-reserve validation
  • Updated every 5 minutes

Verification Process

Reserve balances are validated using secure on-chain monitoring and internal proof-of-reserve systems. Public wallet addresses are not displayed here.

Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Commitment

Your privacy is our priority. swapStream collects minimal data and never sells or shares your information without consent.

What Data We Collect

  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses (temporary)
  • Transaction amounts and pair information
  • IP address (for fraud prevention only)
  • Browser cookies (non-tracking)

What We DON'T Collect

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone)
  • Bank account information
  • Geolocation tracking
  • Behavioral profiling

Data Retention

Transaction records are kept for 90 days for compliance purposes, then permanently deleted. Wallet addresses are never stored after transaction completion.

Security Measures

  • AES-256 encryption for all data in transit
  • No sensitive data stored on servers
  • Regular security audits
  • DDoS protection & rate limiting

Refund Policy

Refunds are only applicable in specific circumstances. Read carefully to understand your rights.

✓ Eligible for Refund

Transaction Failed - Wrong Network

If you sent funds to the wrong blockchain network (e.g., ETH to BTC address)

System Error - Our Fault

If our system failed to process your swap correctly and funds were not received

Duplicate Transactions

If you were charged multiple times due to our processing error

Extreme Rate Volatility

If rates changed >10% between confirmation and processing (rare blockchain delays)

User Error - Wrong address entered
